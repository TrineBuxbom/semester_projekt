GET TO KNOW ME

Hi, I'm Trine Buxbom

I’m currently studying Multimedia design at University College Lillebælt in Odense where I get to unleash this monster of creativity I was born with. I have an enormous passion to create, which is based on my interests for design, photography, and film. I possess a great variety of competences that I know how to use both separately and together.

With graphic design I strive to communicate a message wrapped in the perfectly suited design choices. I have photographed the earth and its people for 8 years and will continue to do so as long as people need memories and the world is beautiful. Video editing is a different story but is also where I’ve unwillingly put my talents since I get to tell stories with moving pictures.
